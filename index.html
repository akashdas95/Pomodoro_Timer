▼

Complete User Guide

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that breaks work into focused intervals (typically 25 minutes) separated by short breaks. It helps improve focus, reduce fatigue, and increase productivity.

How to Use This Timer

  • Select a task from your todo list
  • Click "▶ Start" to begin a 25-minute focus session
  • Work without distractions during the Pomodoro
  • Take a break when the timer ends
  • After 4 sessions, take a longer break

Themes and Music

Choose from 4 calming themes: Calm (lofi beats), Peace (meditation), Serenity (nature sounds), and Zen (relaxation). Music automatically changes when you switch themes.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • SPACE: Start/Pause Timer
  • R: Reset Timer
  • M: Toggle Music
